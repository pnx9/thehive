Basic Static Analysis:

In addition to high entropy[1], the Sample has been detected with a resource as an AutoIt Script[1``], which Malware loves a lot being easy/small to automate the WindowsGUI tasks and other Windows general scripting purposes.

Also another interesting section .text[1```]with a very high entropy and suspicious characteristics such as being Writable/Executable, with the execution -Entry-Point- starting from there.
